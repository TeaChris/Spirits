obatala

Obatala, the sky father. Obatala the maker of man and god alike. Obatala the father of mankind. His eulogies go on and on. He’s the creator of everything to walk earth (man) and everything that descended from heaven ( gods & deities). He is the maker of forms whilst Olodumare is the one who breathes life into his creations. It is this unique power of his that allowed him to trick Aganju into allowing him passage across the great river which Aganju was the ferryman of.

Baba

Baba, the Only Father Figure and confidant of Sango Olukoso. A wise man, steadily guiding Sango to a path of greatness. Never ceases to protect those he cherishes even going as far protecting them with his life.

GBONKA

A Strong man of weapons filled with the intense will to fight anyone who dares to challenge him, sparing none of his enemies. Although he is the left-hand man of the Great Sango Olukoso, he wages war on him, challenging Sango to a great fight after being fortified by the enemies of Sango.

OBA

The first Wife of Sango Olukoso. Beautiful but Irrelevant to the face of the one she Adores; Her husband. With all efforts to win the heart of Sango, she is deceived and banished out of the palace. Wandering in pain, she wails, drenched in the pool of her tears, she turns into the river of her tears know today as Odo Oba (river Oba). Today, she is known as one of the goddess of rivers.

timi OLOFAINA

One of Sango’s most powerful warriors. Second only to Sango and Gbonka when it comes to military might. He was dubbed Olofa ina because his ability to shoot arrows of fire ( find out more about him in the comics). He is Sango’s right hand man.

bashorun oyo Mesi

The Oyo mesi council, represented by their leader; The Bashorun. The Oyo mesi is a council of seven of the most powerful noblemen in the Oyo kingdom. No Alaafin (official title of the ruler of Oyo) could be enthroned or dethroned without prior consent and of performance of rituals by these seven men. They exist to check the power of the king and help him rule the kingdom when the need arises.

olowu oduru

King of Owu Kingdom. A man whose main aim is to Covet the properties and wealth of his cousin, AJAKA. A man who lives lavishly, squandering the resources to further his flashy lifestyle.

osun

The second wife of the Great Sango Olukoso, praised deeply for her culinary skills. Vile, deceitful, and a conceited woman, who cares alone for herself and how Sango sees her. She is the goddess divinity, fertility, beauty, femininity and love.

OYA

She is the goddess of storms and violent weather. She is also the goddess of life and rebirth and holds a lot of power over this process. A savage warrior whose power sweeps all injustice, deceit and dishonesty from her path. A protective mother to her worshippers and Alaafin Sango’s most beloved woman.

baba magbo

These are the special advisers to the Kings of the Oyo kingdom. They share their wisdom with the Kings of the Kingdom.

ajaka

AJAKA The Second Alaafin of the Oyo Kingdom. A simple and smart man trying to maximize the profit and economy of the kingdom. A man who stays clear of battles solely because he has seen war and he doesn’t want war because he believes it claims everything in it’s part. Wanting peace in a world that demanded a strong spine and a lot of Violence was his undoing.

aganju

A great, powerful, and determined god of the volcanoes, deserts, and rivers. Aganju is a force of life that pulls down every obstacle that comes his way. Even though he is a man of great stature, he is also filled with impeccable Rage, solely seeking to destroy Sango Olukoso.

sango

The Great Yoruba god of lightning and thunder. Crowned R uler of the Oyo Kingdom leading us through the pathway to his never-ending feat and strive for power, as he battles the one man that channels his God Mode to be birthed to life.
