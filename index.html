It will include utilities, as well as other benefits.Once all Sango Collection pieces are minted, the next phase of the journey begins.

Why should I support SOA?

The SOA project is a collective of the African spirit, regardless of country, tribe or creed. We believe in the philosophy of “ubuntu”, a Nguni Bantu term meaning "humanity”. For us to thrive, we must believe in ourselves and work together for the future we want to build.

African mythology full of intrigue and wonder, but doesn’t get the recognition it deserves. The continent is brimming with so many beautiful stories about Africa and African mythology, but very often these stories are poorly expressed, or remain untold – our inspiration at SOA is to change that!
